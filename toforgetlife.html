To Forget Life, To Be At Peace , 2020

Composed during the height of the pandemic during my senior year of highschool, this piece was my first completed attempt at writing for my highschool band. Unfortunately circumstances prevented us from ever playing it. Inspired by the works of Oscar Wilde and Walt Whitman, this piece was a reflection on the quiet anger and solemnity of the moment.
